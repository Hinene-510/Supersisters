Where Passion Meets Action

FireSteps is a transformative leadership development bootcamp designed for high school and university students who want to lead with passion and purpose. The program includes three phases: ignite (self-discovery and motivation), challenge (group leadership simulations and outdoor team missions), and impact (designing and leading a mini community project).

Voices That Spark Change
