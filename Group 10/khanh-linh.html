Guiding the Way with Positivity

BrightPath is a mentorship and life-coaching initiative that connects high school students with inspiring university mentors. The program includes bi-weekly mentoring sessions, personal goal mapping, and life skill workshops on topics like time management, emotional intelligence, and values-based decision-making. Each student completes the journey with a written vision statement and action plan. As Captain, I steer the BrightPath community with optimism, ensuring every participant walks away with clarity, confidence, and a mentor for life.
